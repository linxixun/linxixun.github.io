Biography

I am currently a Senior Algorithm Engineer at the Web Search Ranking Strategy Group. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Chuan Zhou and Prof. Bin Wang. My research interests broadly include Graph Representation Learning, Deep Bayesian Learning and Recommender Systems. Now, I am focusing on the frontier exploration and practical application of Large-scale Personalized CTR Models. I have published several papers in top-tier journals and conferences such as IEEE TNNLS, WWW, ICDM, WSDM and SIGIR.

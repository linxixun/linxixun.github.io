Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor in Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS). Before that, I was a senior algorithm engineer in Baidu Inc.. I received my Ph.D. degree from IIE in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Chuan Zhou and Prof. Bin Wang. My research focuses on trustworthy machine learning , with a particular emphasis on trustworthy GNNs, trustworthy LLM-based agents and LLM safety. I have published several papers in top-tier journals and conferences such as IEEE TKDE, IEEE TNNLS, ACM TOIS, ICML, WWW, AAAI, SIGIR and so on. My research has been recognized as Best Paper Candidates, including at ICDM-21 and WWW-21.
Looking for collaborators to do meaningful research. If interested please feel free to contact with me!
